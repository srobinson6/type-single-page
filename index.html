Kalnia

History

Created by Frida Medrano, Kalnia is a delicate, bold font that drew inspiration from the Victorian Era. While it is a lesser known font, Kalnia is best seen for its attention grabbing typography that stands out from other typefaces and is mostly used in poster headlines.
